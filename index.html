FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about BalanceBooks Pro.

Is my data really private?

Absolutely. Your data never leaves your device. We don't have servers storing your financial information. Everything stays local on your computer or browser.

Do I need to connect my bank?

No! Manual entry gives you full control and awareness of every transaction. Many users find this approach helps them stay more engaged with their finances.

Can I use it on multiple devices?

Yes, but each device has its own local data. You can export your data and import it on another device to sync manually.

What if I lose my data?

The app includes built-in backup and restore features. We recommend enabling auto-backup or manually exporting your data regularly.

Is there a subscription?

Never. The web app is free forever. Desktop apps are a one-time purchase with lifetime updates included.

Can I import from other apps?

Yes! You can import transactions from CSV or Excel files. We provide templates to make this easy.
